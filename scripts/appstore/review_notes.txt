ActivityVideoStudio overlays Garmin .FIT activity data onto GoPro .MP4 footage and exports a single MP4.

Sample files to verify all core features (publicly hosted, will remain available):
https://github.com/bokuno-studio/activity-video-studio/releases/tag/app-review-samples-v1
- sample_activity.fit  (Garmin Fenix 8 activity record)
- sample_clip.mp4       (GoPro footage, 60 s, 1080p H.264)

How to test:
1. Launch the app.
2. Drag both files into the main window.
3. The app auto-aligns video and activity by the MP4 creation timestamp; overlays (heart rate, pace, distance, elevation, mini-map) appear immediately.
4. Press Play to preview the synchronized overlay.
5. Use Export to render a final MP4 with overlays burned in.
